Privacy Policy for Birthday Blitz

Last Updated: July 5, 2026

Birthday Blitz ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use our Chrome Extension.

Information We Collect

Birthday Blitz uses Google OAuth to authenticate users and access their Google Calendar. We only request the minimum permissions necessary to function:

  • Read-only access to view your calendars (to allow you to select which calendar to use).
  • Write access to create events (to add birthdays to your selected calendar).

How We Use Your Information

We do not store, share, or sell your personal data on external servers. All operations are performed directly between your browser and Google's APIs. Your calendar data remains on your Google account and is not sent to any third-party databases owned by us.

Third-Party Access

We do not share your calendar data or personal information with any third parties.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website at https://tobyyeung.github.io/.
